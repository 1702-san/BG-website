ABOUT



BUG HUNTERS is a community constituting students who are interested in Google Developer Technologies. The primary goal of a BUG HUNTERS Chapter is to assist other students to advance in various fields by organizing various events in associated universities. They play a key role in creating solutions for local businesses and communities. In turn, this creates ample opportunities for people to share their dreams and ambitions as well as to learn several things as they proceed.
